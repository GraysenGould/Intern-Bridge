It's great to meet you! Let's get this interview started. Here are some questions to help us learn more about your experience and skills:

1. Can you tell me a bit about your background and what led you to pursue a career in technology?

2. What programming languages are you most comfortable with, and why do you prefer them?

3. Can you describe a challenging technical problem you encountered in a previous project and how you resolved it?

4. How do you approach learning new technologies or programming languages? Can you give an example of a new skill you've recently acquired?

5. Teamwork is essential in tech projects. Can you share an experience where you collaborated with others to achieve a common goal?

6. What’s your experience with version control systems, such as Git? How do you use them in your development process?

7. How do you ensure the quality and maintainability of your code?

8. Have you had any experience with cloud computing platforms (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, etc.)? If so, can you describe a project where you utilized one of these platforms?

9. Can you discuss a time when you had to communicate complex technical concepts to a non-technical audience? How did you approach it?

10. Finally, what excites you most about the opportunity to work at our company, and how do you see yourself contributing to our team? 

Thank you for your responses!
